About Still & Wild

Still & Wild is a collaborative webzine born from our shared love for nature, creativity, and reflection. We believe that quiet moments — like walking through forests or sketching under trees — hold healing power.

This space invites readers to slow down, to listen to the rustle of leaves, to notice shifting light, to rediscover small beauties often overlooked.

Who We Are

We are a group of friends, artists, writers, and quiet observers who find joy in the subtle details of the world around us. This project emerged from our shared longing for calm and connection — with ourselves, with nature, and with each other.

Each of us brings a different voice to the zine: some of us carry sketchbooks into the forest, others collect sounds or memories. Together, we weave these threads into a digital tapestry of stillness and wildness.

Why We Created This

In a world that often rushes forward, Still & Wild is our way of stepping sideways — into a slower, softer pace. We hope this space reminds you that even a single breath, a birdsong, or a fallen leaf can be a starting point for wonder.
